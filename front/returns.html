Delivery and Returns

Shipping

Packages are generally sent 3 days after payment. We use Colissimo services, which provide you with a tracking number (signature required). The tracking link is available in your Customer account. Shipping fees include preparation and packaging costs. These are fixed costs, whereas delivery depends on the weight of the package.

Orders are shipped from France. For deliveries outside of the European Union (including DROM-COM), items are sold excluding French VAT, customs fees and import taxes may be charged to the customer.

Return

MieMie must be informed of return within 15 days after the reception of the order. The parcel must be shipped within 30 days after its reception. With the exception of a mistake in preparation, return shipping fees are at the expense of the customer.

Other than defective items, all returned articles must be received in their new and original condition to be considered for a refund or exchange. Original packaging and labels must be accompanied with the package.

  • In their brand new state: Not worn, especially no folds on the leather, nor traces on the soles.
  • Returned in their original packaging and in new condition.

We recommend that you send us the articles by registered package with acknowledgment of receipt. Otherwise, MieMie cannot be responsible for loss, refund, or exchange.

Please indicate your name and your order reference with your package. Our return address is:

For returns from countries outside the European Union

Put into a transparent pocket sticked on the package the following documents:

  • For your customs form, please indicate "Return" on the form.
  • Add 3 copies of the order's invoice.
  • Add a copy of the customs fee receipt you received with your package, which indicates you already paid the customs.

If the above conditions are not met, MieMie reserves the right to refuse the package and return it to its sender.

Exchange

For exchanges, we send the new item upon receipt of the original purchase at our expense from our warehouse, within the limit of one exchange.

For metropolitan France, delivery costs fixed at 10 Euros including VAT will be charged to the Customer for each delivery from the second exchange of the same order. For European Union countries, these costs will be 15€. For other countries, these costs will be 25€.

The return is at the expense of the customer, as indicated in the previous section. Please inform us as soon as possible about the desired article for a booking.
